##  Key Business Insights
- Business Class is the main revenue contributor.
- Peak departure times generate higher average prices.
- Premium airlines sustain higher prices due to brand positioning.
- Non-stop flights significantly increase ticket value.
- Metro routes represent strong business demand.

---

##  Revenue Strategy Recommendations
- Apply dynamic pricing during peak hours.
- Expand business class offerings on high-demand routes.
- Increase upselling from Economy to Business.
- Use value-added services instead of discounts.
- Optimize pricing per route and departure time.
